How to generate your ZIP
  1. 1
    Open the converter — Visit pdf2cbt.vercel.app in your browser.
  2. 2
    Upload your exam PDF — Drop in any question-paper PDF that has multiple-choice questions.
  3. 3
    Configure & convert — Set section names, marking scheme (+3 / −1 for BITSAT), and click Generate ZIP.
  4. 4
    Download the ZIP — The site produces a .zip containing data.json and question images. Save it to your device.
  5. 5
    Upload below — Drag & drop or browse to select the ZIP file. ExamCBT will load it as a full mock test instantly.
